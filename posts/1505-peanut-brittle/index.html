Peanut Brittle

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 290 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 220 reviews
Yield : 8

Ingredients :

  • 3 c. white sugar
  • 1/2 c. water
  • 3 tsp. butter
  • 2 tsp. soda
  • 1 c. white Karo
  • 3 c. raw peanuts
  • 1 tsp. salt

Directions :

  • Boil sugar, Karo and water until it spins thread.
  • Add peanuts and stir continually after peanuts are added. Cook until mixture turns brownish gold. Take from the fire. Add butter, salt and soda.
  • Pour onto buttered pan to cool.
  • When hard, break into pieces.
