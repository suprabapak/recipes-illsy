Strawberry Yum-Yum

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 197 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 11 reviews
Yield : 3

Ingredients :

  • 1 stick margarine
  • 2 c. graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 pkg. Dream Whip
  • 1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
  • 1 c. sugar
  • 1 c. cold milk
  • 2 c. strawberries

Directions :

  • Melt margarine and stir in crumbs.
  • Place half of mixture in bottom of baking dish.
  • Whip Dream Whip and cream cheese with sugar and cold milk.
  • Pour half of the Dream Whip mixture on crumbs.
  • Spread strawberries on creamed mixture.
  • Spread remainder of creamed mixture on top of strawberries.
  • Sprinkle remainder of crumbs on top.
  • Chill 3 hours or longer.
