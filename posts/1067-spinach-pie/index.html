Spinach Pie

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 224 calories,
4 stars - based on 281 reviews
Yield : 8

Ingredients :

  • pizza dough enough for 2 layers
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 stick margarine
  • 1 lb. Ricotta
  • 1 lb. Mozzarella
  • 1 pkg. frozen chopped spinach, drained
  • 1/2 c. grated Parmesan cheese optional
  • 1 small can sauce pizza, tomato or spaghetti

Directions :

  • Put 1/2 pizza dough on greased sheet.
  • Spread sauce to cover dough.
  • Saute onion and garlic in butter; mix in cheeses and spinach.
  • Spread on pizza dough.
  • Place second layer of dough on top.
  • Bake at 450u00b0 for 15 to 20 minutes.
