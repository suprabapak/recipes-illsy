Cranberry-Pecan Bars

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 302 calories,
4.2 stars - based on 253 reviews
Yield : 3

Ingredients :

  • 1 1/2 c. flour
  • 2 Tbsp. sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/3 c. margarine
  • 1 c. finely chopped pecans
  • 1 1/4 c. sugar
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 2 Tbsp. milk
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 c. cranberries, cut small
  • 1/2 c. coconut

Directions :

  • Mix flour, sugar and salt.
  • Cut in margarine and stir in 1/2 cup of nuts.
  • Press into 9 x 13-inch pan.
  • Bake at 350u00b0 for about 15 minutes.
  • Mix 1 1/4 cups sugar and 2 tablespoons flour.
  • Stir in beaten eggs, milk, salt and vanilla.
  • Fold in cranberries, coconut and the rest of the nuts.
  • Spread over partly baked crust. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, until top is golden.
  • Cool in pan 10 minutes on a rack; cut in bars while warm.
