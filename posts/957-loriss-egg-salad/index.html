Loris S Egg Salad

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 270 calories,
4.7 stars - based on 51 reviews
Yield : 8

Ingredients :

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1 small jar pimento
  • 1/3 c. Miracle Whip
  • 1 chopped sweet gherkin pickle
  • 1/4 c. chopped onion
  • 1/4 c. chopped green pepper
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • dash of paprika

Directions :

  • With a potato masher, mix eggs, onion, pepper and pickle. Stir in mayonnaise.
  • Add drained pimentos and seasonings.
  • Chill and serve.
