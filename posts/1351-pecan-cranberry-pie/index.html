Pecan Cranberry Pie

January 1, 0001



Nutrition : 255 calories,
5 stars - based on 222 reviews
Yield : 6

Ingredients :

  • 4 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 2/3 c. sugar
  • 2 Tbsp. melted margarine
  • 1 c. dark corn syrup
  • 1 c. coarsely chopped cranberries
  • 1 c. chopped pecans
  • 1 9-inch unbaked pastry shell

Directions :

  • Beat eggs, sugar, margarine and corn syrup until blended. Stir in cranberries and pecans.
  • Pour into unbaked pastry shell. Cover edge with 2 inches foil.
  • Bake on lowest rack of oven at 350u00b0 for 60 to 70 minutes.
  • Take foil off after 45 minutes of baking.
